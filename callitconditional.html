Call It Conditional

The blue colors reference the stereotypical un- emotional view on love and today's culture, the red references the love aspect, A.K.A. the female nurturer way of viewing love and then in the middle you generally see the color "Purple" this references the Lust factor in a relationship, each image is depicting a different outcome and stereotype in this hook-up culture. It's more of a personal view on how we experience love, what is it? Do we even know how to love, feminism says no! Because we are not taught how to love, we are taught how to A) nurture(women) and B) support(men).
